\section{Part I: Unsupervised Analysis}
\subsection{Genre Proportions}
The labeled dataset is moderately unbalanced. Classical is the largest class with 29.82\% of the observations, followed by Jazz with 26.19\%, Rock with 16.88\%, Blues with 13.82\%, and Pop with 13.29\%.

\subsection{Hierarchical Clustering and Silhouettes}
Hierarchical clustering and silhouette diagnostics can be applied to the same standardized acoustic descriptors to compare data-driven groupings with the five observed genre labels.

\begin{equation}
\mathrm{sil}(x_i) = \frac{b_i - a_i}{\max(a_i, b_i)}
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
a_i = \frac{1}{|C_k|-1}\sum_{x_\ell \in C_k, \ell \neq i} d(x_i, x_\ell),
\qquad
b_i = \min_{k' \neq k}\frac{1}{|C_{k'}|}\sum_{x_\ell \in C_{k'}} d(x_i, x_\ell)
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
\widehat{K} = \arg\max_{K \in \{2,\ldots,10\}} \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\mathrm{sil}(x_i)
\end{equation}
